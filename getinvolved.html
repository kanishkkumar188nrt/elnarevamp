Partners

This is why your company should partner with Us

For the first time in India, we are creating a platform of children and teachers at large will get together at various educational events in different parts of the country to share their experiences to inspire and transform the world around them. ELNA is about Bringing Education Live. Students and teachers from different parts of the India will share their experiences and on how they are learning to connect their academics with their social environment and successfully moving in their lives to a level of personal and academic satisfaction, achievement and success. You will have the opportunity to present your product and/or service to an incredible Captive audience comprising affluent Target Group with high disposable income mostly in the SEC B to SEC A+ range residing in various metros of India. You also get the opportunity to engage with the key influencers in every household i.e. school children in the age group of 10-14 years. Don’t miss this opportunity. Write to us at contact@elna.co.in to partner with us for our various programs.
