Upcoming Events

  • Dastak-a clarion call by young minds.
    • Dastak These workshops are window to understanding the immense potential of Inquiry Based Learning. It’s a systematic and logical process of classroom workshops integrated with interactive sessions where students slowly but steadily sensitized to their social environment.
  • Workshop I
    • It is an introduction to the themes of Dastak 2014 at various schools of Gurgaon. Understanding WHY these social problems are a threat to our individual existence.
  • Workshop II
    • It is an attempt to help children collate their ideas and thoughts together in a logical manner so that they can come up with the message which makes the society at large aware of these social problems. This is termed as Art of Expressing.
  • Workshop III
    • It is about converting the story into a script so that students learn to use the Noncognitive & Cognitive skills together. This workshop will help students learn the first step to Performing art.
