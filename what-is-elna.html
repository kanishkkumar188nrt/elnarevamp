Introduction

ELNA believes in life skill development through integrated learning creating value based education for children. We enable the students to understand, devise and develop and finally apply the knowledge thus acquired to solve any problem .


Mission

Revealing the secrets of life through nature based activities, ELNA is an endeavor at turning the child from a passive learner into an active learner . ELNA enables the child to be a constructor of knowledge rather than a mere recipient of knowledge which is largely made available to him or her through the printed media and ‘the banking education’ methodology preached in schools.


Objective

The Experiential Learning via Nature Based Activities (ELNA), format aims at integrating every subject with a nature based activity giving hands on experience while ensuring:

  • The child understands the concepts taught in class by relating them to the experience he or she has undergone during the activity.
  • This methodology helps   her/him explain the relationships between cause and effect for any action.
  • The process of Observation and Exploration eventually leads to Expression of knowledge which comes from within, rather being forced from outside, by means of memorizing pieces of disintegrated information, which sometimes the learner may not be able to assimilate at that moment or take years, before understanding the full purport of the subject thus taught
