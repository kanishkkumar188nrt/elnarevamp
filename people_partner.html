PEOPLE

Core Team

  • Surekha Waldia

    ELNA is a passion which has come up after dreaming and wondering in classrooms as a child student, wondering why can’t we take so-called education beyond classrooms and connect it with the social environment. This inquisitiveness made Surekha pursue Non Profit Leadership (NPL) course at University of Pennsylvania. After coming back , she decided to help today’s young generation understand the importance of application based methodology and how to reach the root cause of a problem, which she herself understood after completing 20 years of formal education and more than six plus years of work in corporate and social sector. In 2012, ELNA was started with the objective of bringing Education Live in the classrooms. Surekha is an MBA from Symbiosis Institute of Management Studies, Pune and MS in Non-profit Leadership from University of Pennsylvania. She has a research paper on WFP published as a monograph in 2011- ‘History of Present’ Human Resource Crisis at World Food Programme. Surekha is passionate about writing in the field of education and loves gardening as her hobby.

  • Savitri Waldia

    Savitri always wanted to be an IAS officer but ended up being a teacher which she resisted in the beginning only to discover later that teaching was not a profession for me but a passion .And it took her so many years to undo the layers of influence exerted by her surroundings. Often passion is mixed with ambition. Passion is like a perennial fountain whereas ambition is like a rain fed river. She chose to be with her passion not ambition. Savitri has been in teaching profession for more than 30 years with the experience of teaching from class prep to senior secondary classes she brings in with her a rich and varied experience leading to the point when one can actually fathom the true meaning of teaching and its nuances. Learning from children, learning from the varied environment they come from and the challenges emerging out of overcrowded classrooms and over loaded curriculum along with many other well known problems. She truly believes in finding a way out of all these problems via her teaching methods. Savitri found herself getting closer and closer to ELNA because she could see the sprouts of some real learning taking place .ELNA is on its way to do what she has always been thinking of . That’s why Savitri decided to connect herself and move along on the journey with the hope that one day these little sprouts will grow into plants and into a full fledged crop in time to come. Savitri Waldia is MA in English and loves to write on school education in her free time.

  • Dinakshi Arora

    Dinakshi Arora is a writer, blogger and poet with an innate flair for expression. She has worked extensively in the field of content and creative writing & editing. Dinakshi is passionate about facilitating ability of expression and creativity with words in children. Her previous experience in IT industry with organizations like IBM, Polaris, Satyam and HR industry adds to her multitude of versatility. Dinakshi is associated with ELNA as working towards practical education that has real impact on society is a subject close to her heart. ELNA provides a platform for students to engage with social ecosystem actively and prepares them to be aware, conscious and responsible citizens of tomorrow.

  • Ela Gupta

    Ela Gupta is a Marketing and communications professional who has previously worked with Synergy Consulting and Adfactors PR. Since 2013, she has established herself as an Independent Consultant in the field of Marketing and Communications. She is associated with CSR initiatives, social enterprises and NGOs and help in designing their programs and marketing initiatives. She is also associated with Art & Culture projects and handles their PR & Media aspect. She has experience in strategizing media and communications functions of corporates planning to raise funds from conceptualization phase to the implementation phase. She has been involved in the areas of content creation, event planning, media monitoring and research. She has worked on multiple clients during her corporate stint including India's largest IPO till date - Coal India Limited. She is an alumnus of Miranda House and Birla Institute of Management Technology. She has done additional courses in International Journalism & Global Communications from London School of Economics and Media Laws from NALSAR, Hyderabad. Ela is associated with ELNA as she believes in the importance of creative education and how thinking out of box and stepping out of classrooms time and again help kids develop more.
